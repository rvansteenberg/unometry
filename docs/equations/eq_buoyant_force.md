
> [!abstract]+ Buoyancy Formula Triangle (Click to Expand)
> $$\begin{array}{c} F_b \\ \hline \rho \quad \vert \quad V \quad \vert \quad g \end{array}$$
> *Note: This is a four-variable relationship. To find any one value, cover it and solve for the others.*
> - **$F_b = \rho \cdot V \cdot g$** (Buoyant Force)
> - **$\rho = F_b / (V \cdot g)$** (Density of Fluid)
> - **$V = F_b / (\rho \cdot g)$** (Volume of Displaced Fluid)
> - **$g = F_b / (\rho \cdot V)$** (Gravitational Acceleration)
