
- **Direction [Inward]:** Drilling (Increases Density, Decreases Volume).
    
- **Direction [Outward]:** Driving (Decreases Density, Increases Volume).
    
- **The Over-arching Constant:** Every "Drill" happens inside a "Drive." You cannot have a nested volume without a container volume.

| **Outward** | **D.R.I.V.E.** | **D**imensional **R**esolution **I**nter-scalar **V**olume **E**xpansion | Expanding into the parent container to understand the "Big Picture."   |
| **Inward**  | **D.R.I.L.L.** | **D**imensional **R**esolution **I**ntra-scalar **L**ocal **L**imitation | Collapsing into the internal structure to find the "Resolution Floor." |
